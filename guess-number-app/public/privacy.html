Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Guess Number ("the Game") is a number guessing game. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

  • Google Account Info: If you sign in with Google, we only store a unique user ID and your chosen display name. We do not store your name, email, or any other Google account information.
  • Game Data: We store your game statistics (wins, guess counts) to display on the leaderboard. You can opt out of the leaderboard at any time in your profile settings, which deletes your game statistics.
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how the Game is used (e.g., which features are popular). Analytics is only enabled with your consent. No personally identifiable information is shared with Google Analytics unless you have given consent.

Cookies and Consent

The Game asks for your consent before enabling analytics cookies. You can accept or decline when first visiting. Google Analytics cookies are only set if you accept. Your consent preference is stored locally in your browser.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your location, contacts, photos, or any device sensor data.
  • We do not sell, share, or trade your personal information with third parties.

Data Storage and Retention

Your data is stored on our server and is not shared with any third parties. Guest users' data is not stored. Account data is retained until you delete your account.

Your Rights

  • You can play as a guest without signing in — no data is stored.
  • You can change your display name at any time.
  • You can opt out of the leaderboard, which deletes your game statistics.
  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from your profile settings.
  • You can accept or decline analytics cookies. Declining does not affect gameplay.
  • If you are in the EU, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data under the GDPR.

Children's Privacy

The Game is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us at flamebots.org.
