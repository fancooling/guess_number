Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Guess Number ("the Game") is a number guessing game. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

  • Google Account Info: If you sign in with Google, we only store a unique user ID and your chosen display name. We do not store your name, email, or any other Google account information.
  • Game Data: We store your game statistics (wins, guess counts) for the leaderboard. You can opt out anytime in your profile, which deletes your game statistics.
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how the Game is used. Analytics is only enabled with your consent.

Cookies and Consent

The Game asks for your consent before enabling analytics cookies. You can accept or decline. Your preference is stored locally in your browser.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your location, contacts, photos, or device sensor data.
  • We do not sell, share, or trade your personal information with third parties.

Data Storage and Retention

Your data is stored on our server and is not shared with third parties. Guest users' data is not stored. Account data is retained until you delete your account.

Your Rights

  • You can play as a guest without signing in — no data is stored.
  • You can change your display name at any time.
  • You can opt out of the leaderboard, which deletes your game statistics.
  • You can delete your account and all data from your profile settings.
  • You can accept or decline analytics cookies. Declining does not affect gameplay.
  • EU users have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of personal data under the GDPR.

Children's Privacy

The Game is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact

If you have questions or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us at flamebots.org.
